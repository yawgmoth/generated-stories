Yoda and Leia are married , and Yoda 's mother , Jabba , lives with them . Yoda and Leia quarrel regarding Jabba , and Yoda is forced to choose between Jabba and Leia . Yoda 's wife , Leia , dies . Leia , Yoda 's wife , on her death bed , has Yoda promise that he will marry Vader , Leia 's best friend Vader loved Yoda before he married Leia . Yoda , marrying a widow , Vader , discovers that he has `` caught a tartar '' and has wedded a domestic tyrant . Yoda becomes the second husband of Vader , whose first husband , Dooku , had mysteriously disappeared and was supposed to be dead . After Yoda and Vader are married , Vader 's first husband , Dooku , appears secretly to Yoda . Vader , Yoda 's tyrannical wife , goes out for the evening and orders Yoda not to leave the house while she is away . Vader , a termagant , supposes herself a widow and marries Yoda . Dooku , Vader 's undivorced husband , appears secretly to Yoda , Vader 's present husband , and tries craftily to get Yoda to pay him money for not claiming Vader as his wife . Vader , while too severely reprimanding her husband , Yoda , bursts a blood vessel and expires . Yoda finds that a delusion regarding the apparition of his deceased wife , Vader , is entangled with truth . Yoda hears the voice of his deceased wife , Vader , and there seems absolutely no doubt that it is her voice . Yoda believes he sees the apparition of his dead wife , Vader . Yoda struggles to overtake the fleeing apparition of Vader , falls from a cliff and meets his death .
